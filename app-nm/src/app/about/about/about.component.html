About this website
How it started

When my job hunt began shortly after finishing my undergraduate degree, I came to the realization that like artists, web developers often have portfolios as well.

Although my journey has only begun, I figured I should start building my own portfolio and add on to it as I developed more skills and experience. It could be a place for me to easily showcase who I am, and what I aspire to be. A place where I could keep a log of what everything I have learnt and will learn.
